About Us

In the middle of October 2013, after years of experience in the Media field, we established our first company.

We wrote and shot videos, we performed and produced podcasts, and we learned something new along every step of the way.

During this journey, we discovered fresh, young talents around the world and our small initial team grew into a vast network expanding over America, Europe and Asia. We learned that borders cannot limit our collaborations.

Finally, our efforts led to an exciting transformation, and in April of 2017, M Studios was born. We would like to share with you many years of experience and knowledge in Media Production and accompany you on your creative journey.
